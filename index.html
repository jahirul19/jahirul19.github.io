Welcome to
Pro Bike FC

Pro Bike FC was set up under the principle of making every rider feel they are being listened to. We do not gauge our level of interest in you based on if you are a pro cyclist or a novice, we just love talking bikes, and we love spending our time teaching everything bike! We are 100% a female friendly store, and Pro Bike FC is always on the lookout for new women team riders, of all levels to throw on the kit and hit it up! Pro Bike FC sponsors and assists a women only team and the more, the merrier.

We encourage all levels of riders to meet up on our Sunday rides, speak to us about our coaching clinics both on and off the bike and the many other services Pro Bike FC and our affiliated NPC Training Systems have to offer.

Please, drop us a line or keep an eye out on our Face Book page. Drop in any time, or just come on down for a shop ride. Either way, we would LOVE to see you.
